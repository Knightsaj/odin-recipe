Old Fashioned Pancakes


Description

Pancakes are a great choice for breakfast. It's prep and cook time make it a good choice. It's great having it with eggs or by itself.

Ingredients (per 1 serving)

  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 3/8 tsp sugar
  • 2 1/2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp butter, melted

Steps (in order)

  1. Get a large bowl
  2. Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
  3. Pour in the milk and egg
  4. Melt the butter and pour into the bowl and mix until smooth
  5. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium heat
  6. Pour or scoop the batter onto the pan, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake
  7. Brown on both sides and serve hot
